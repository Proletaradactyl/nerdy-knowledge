How to Play

  1. Click the start button.
  2. You will be prompted to answer 15 nerdy questions.
  3. If your answer turns green you are correct!
  4. If the answer you selected turns red, better luck next time!
  5. After you have answered all the questions you will be prompted to add your name to the leaderboard.
